Introduction

Last updated on Jan. 9, 2016 (replaced the compressed folder).

This material is intended as a course supplement or independent-study source for college students (undergraduate and graduate) who have taken courses in abstract algebra (linear algebra and group theory) and modern geometry (affine, projective, finite).

It may also interest some professional mathematicians, since the content has not been published previously except as follows.

A preliminary version was distributed at the 1976 summer AMS-MAA joint summer meetings in Toronto. Excerpts were published in the journal Computer Graphics and Art (Vol. 2, No. 1, February 1977, pp. 5-7).

Additional notes were distributed informally, and are available as Notes on Groups and Geometry, 1978-1986 (pdf ). These and later notes are, at this writing, on the Web at http://finitegeometry.org/sc/map.html. This book is based on the notes at that site.
